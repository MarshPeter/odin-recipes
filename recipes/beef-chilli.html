Beef Chilli

Description

A simple chicken taco recipe that is both filling and healthy.

Ingredients

Chilli

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 2 tablespoons chilli powder, or to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground chipotle
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt, plus more to taste
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 12 ounces hazy IPA beer
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced fire-roasted tomatoes with green chillies
  • 2 (16 ounce jars) extra chunky salsa
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans pinto beans

Toppings

  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup sliced onions
  • 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro

Steps

  1. Add beef to a dry cold pot, and turn heat to high.
  2. When beef begins to sizzle, break up into small pieces with a potato masher or spatula. Continue to cook and break up beef until no longer pink, about 7 minutes.
  3. Add chilli powder, black pepper, salt, and cinnammon to the pot. Cook stirring for 1 minute, then pour in beer.
  4. Stir, scraping the brown bits from the bottom of the pot as you do. Let mixture boil until liquid is reduced by about half, 2 to 3 minutes.
  5. Add sugar, oregano, garlic powder, diced tomatoes and jarred salsa. Stir in; return mixture to a simmer.
  6. Stir in beans, reduce heat to medium-low, and cook, stirring occasionally, until chilli is thickened and flavour is developed to your liking, about 1 hour.
  7. Taste, asjust the seasoning. Top with sour cream, green onions, cheddar cheese, and cilantro as required.
