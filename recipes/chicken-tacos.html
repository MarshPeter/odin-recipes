Chicken Tacos

Description

A simple chicken taco recipe that is both filling and healthy.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons taco seasoning mix
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Combine chicken broth and taco seasoning min in a bowl.
  2. Place chicken in a slow cooker. Pour Chicken broth mixture over chicken.
  3. Cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours. Shread Chicken
